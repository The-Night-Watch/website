Photography

Still Life

A selection of various photographs I made over the years, either for clients or for myself. One of my favourite subject is architecture and I have been particularely fascinated by the buildings of Prague when I moved there in 2018, so many of the pictures from this album were taken there. The mix of Renaissance revivalist, modernist, and Art Nouveau works makes the city one of the most interesting places in Europe for architecture enthusiasts, and I discovered interesting buildings on a weekly basis.
