The Astronomy Club (2020)

Poster design

The
Astronomy
Club

The Astronomy Club is a series of posters, in collaboration with graphic designer Florent Bodart exploring the theme of space exploration in the International Typographic Style. Some of the posters were sold on the Habitat website. The full series will soon be available on The Astronomy Club official website.

The project started as nine posters, one for each planet of the solar system. We then explored the theme further with posters depicting NASA's missions and aircrafts, and ended up with close to fifty different designs. We used the treasure trove that is NASA's public domain image archives to create modern homages to classic space missions and technologies.

We wanted to explore the different poster printing techniques available. Some of the posters were designed to be riso printed, while others are screen printed. We tried to use each technique's strength to create iconic, bold pieces. We also expanded the collection by using different languages and alphabets.
