Space Forge (2020)

3D modeling

Space Forge

Space Forge is a British company offering micro gravity manufacturing solutions through small, returnable satellites. I worked on abstract 3D illustrations representing the three industries the company is involved in: electronics, pharmaceuticals, and alloys. The illustrations needed to be realistic but abstract enough to represent any of their client.

I used Blender for modeling and rendering the illustrations. I experimented a lot with materials, textures and lighting, trying to emulate real life materials, but also creating new ones by a process of trial and error.

This project presented an interesting opportunity to express myself in a new way, at least new to me. Creating abstract, otherwordly objects that still look believably photorealistic. Having to deal with such subjects, lighting and composition became an integral aspect of the creative process. Not unlike a real world product photo shoot, I used multiple points ligthing system as well as virtual light reflectors.
