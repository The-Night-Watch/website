La Cite (2020)

Animated film and soundtrack

La Cite

La Cité is an ongoing concept album project. Made out of twelve tracks, each one a chapter of an overarching story. The music video of each song, when put together in order, forms an hour long animated movie about the trip of a music composer to a far away planet. I work on it as a low priority side project do I do not have a planned release date yet.

The sound of La Cité is polymorphic, mixing analog synthetizers, digital voice sampling, and live instruments recordings, to create a changing soundscape, following the vicissitudes of the composer’s trip across the mysterious planet. Early in his exploration of the seemingly desert planet, during a survey of the local flora, the composer discovers a walled city, inhabited by strange mask wearing creatures. The relationship between the composer and the indigenous population will serve as the canvas upon which La Cité will develop its musical themes.

The city’s architecture is inspired by the work of Claude Nicolas Ledoux and the paintings of Giorgio de Chirico. I also drew inspiration from my own dreams for the design of the city, which I wanted to keep Earth-like, playing with the monumentality of classical architecture to give it a familiar yet sacred appearance.

Every building in the desert city is designed according to the same 3D grid, giving a visual coherence to the architecture.
