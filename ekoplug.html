Ekoplug (2020)

Branding and 3D modeling

Ekoplug

Ekoplug is a French electric car charger installer. I was in charge of their brand identity. Ekoplug offers services for private individuals, medium scale co-owned buildings, as well as companies. I came up with a design system that could adapt to each of those audiences in a timeless style.

The main challenge of the project was to establish Ekoplug, a new company, as a reliable brand in a technical industry. To do so, I chose to keep their identity simple and timeless, with bold, recognizable colors.
